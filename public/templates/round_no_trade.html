Catan Marketplace
Rules of the marketplace.
4 to 1
All players have the ability to trade any four (4) of the same resource for another resource of their choosing.
3 to 1
Players who have built on any intersection of a 3:1 harbor can trade any three (3) of the same resource for another resource of their choosing.

In this example, you can build at either A or B to receive the 3 to 1 bonus.
2 to 1
Players who have built on any intersection of a 2:1 harbor can trade any two (2) of that specific resource for another resource of their choosing.

In this example, you can build at either A or B to receive the 2 to 1 bonus for Grain.
