Setup Phase Building
During the Setup Phase of the game, you can place a single settlement and road during your turn. Simply drag and drop each onto any valid building spot as show by the ghost images.
Building a Settlement
To build a settlement, you need 1 Brick, 1 Lumber, 1 Grain and 1 Sheep.

You can only build a settlement on an intersection that is connected by one of your roads. That intersection must be at least 1 full intersection away from any other building. The ghost images in the example below are valid spots for player red to build a settlement:

Building a Road
To build a road, you need 1 Brick and 1 Lumber.
